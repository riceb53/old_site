The value I have selected is fairness. I think at their base, most other values come down to fairness. I think that the greatest gains in the world can be achieved by making the world more fair. I do have a distinct idea of what fairness is though. To me, fairness is treating people the same and helping everyone regardless of skill level.

The last topic someone asked me for advice on was Math. I am a math teacher, and my job is to help students learn math. This did not have to do with nay of my values but I do try to treat my students fairly. Often, at my school well behaved students will be ignored because they aren't 'a problem' but I try to make sure to help everyone.

I think my values can help mitigate stereotype threat. Since I believe that everyone should be treated the same, there is no reason to treat others or have others treat me differently because of any particular skill or attribute that I have. If I begin to feel steretotype threat at DBC I plan to simply focus on the work and prove that I can handle it. Deep down I do believe I can and it is simply a matter of focusing and putting in the time required. In the past, I have read certain books which calm me down and put my struggles in perspective. I plan on reading a few pages of them each day to help my mood.
